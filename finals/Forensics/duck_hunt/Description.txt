Challenge: Duck Hunt
Creator: nmluan

Description:
Yata the Crow, a long-time adversary of the Apples Institution, had been
secretly plotting his magnum opus: unleashing one million ducks across the
campus, exploiting the well-documented inability of literal adults to
resist the temptation of hunting ducks.

The Supreme Universal Duck Organisation (SUDO) caught wind of the plan and
moved to contain the situation. Realising he had been compromised, Yata
hastily deleted every incriminating file from his $3 laptop and handed
the drive over without a fight, apparently confident that deletion meant
gone forever.

SUDO operatives imaged the drive before releasing it. Mounting it showed
five mundane files and nothing else. But a forensics analyst noticed something
in the directory listing that didn't quite add up.

Your mission: examine the raw disk image, recover what Yata tried to erase,
and find the flag hidden inside.
