Guide:
1. `recording_1.mp4` is "A Town with an Ocean View".
2. The piece is from Kiki's Delivery Service and is credited to Joe Hisaishi.
3. Joe Hisaishi's date of birth is 1950-12-06, so part 1 is `a_town_with_an_ocean_view_1950_12_06`.
4. `recording_2.mp4` is "Nhat Ky Cua Me", performed by Hien Thuc and written by Nguyen Van Chung.
5. The song's official video is known for sand-painting visuals, and the single was released on 2012-03-03.
6. Part 2 is `sand_painting_2012_03_03`.
7. Join both parts in order with a single underscore.
